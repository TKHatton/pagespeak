Welcome to PageSpeak

Your dyslexia-first reading companion. Read any webpage or PDF aloud with word tracking, focus tools, and an AI sidekick.

1

Highlight and Read

Select any text on a webpage. A speaker button will appear. Click it to hear the text read aloud. Or press Alt+S. No selection? It reads the whole page.

2

Read PDFs Too

Right-click any PDF link and choose "Open PDF in PageSpeak Reader." PageSpeak extracts the text and lets you read it aloud with all the same tools.

3

Customize Your Experience

Click the PageSpeak icon to open settings. Choose your voice, adjust speed, enable word tracking, switch to a dyslexia-friendly font (OpenDyslexic, Atkinson Hyperlegible, or Lexend), adjust letter spacing, and more.

4

Ask the AI Sidekick

Open the side panel to summarize, simplify, or ask questions about what you just read. Choose a persona lens to change how the AI responds. Works with local AI (Ollama) or cloud AI (Claude).

What You Can Do

Word-by-Word Tracking

Each word highlights as the voice reads it. Choose your highlight color from 6 presets.

Focus Lens

A bar at the top of the screen shows the current sentence in large, clear text.

Reading Ruler

A line guide follows your cursor to help you track which line you're reading.

Distraction Dimming

Dims everything except what's being read so you can focus.

Dyslexia-Friendly Fonts

Switch any webpage to OpenDyslexic, Atkinson Hyperlegible, or Lexend with one click.

Letter & Line Spacing

Widen letter and line spacing — the most research-backed reading aid for dyslexia.

Color Overlay

Apply a tinted overlay to reduce visual stress. Choose from 5 colors.

PDF Reader

Open any PDF in PageSpeak's reader. Extracts text and enables all reading tools.
